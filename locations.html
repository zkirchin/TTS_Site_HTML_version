Asheville

Asheville - better known amongst the TTS team as "Beer City" - is amazing for so many reasons. With 50 local beers at your fingertips, it's no wonder Asheville has become such a hotspot for techies and start-ups. And if a cold one isn't your thing, the mountains make for beautiful vistas and wicked awesome outdoor activities. Whether you want to jump on with one of Asheville’s awesome web development shops, work alongside other freelance developers living the dream, or start your own thing, Asheville is a great place to begin your journey.

Our full-time classes are hosted out of Mojo Coworking in downtown Asheville and our part-time classes are located in Asheville's Center for Professional Studies.

Check out Pop Up Code in Asheville which begins September 7th.

Our next Code Immersion Cohort commences January 2016

 

 
 

 

Atlanta

Tech Talent South's Atlanta code immersion programs are hosted at Strongbox West, in the heart of trendy West Midtown. At almost 50,000 square feet, it's the biggest and baddest coworking space in the Southeast. With everything from endless meeting rooms to a video arcade... and best of all, free parking, we're proud to call Strongbox West home. Just a hop, skip, and a jump away is Georgia Tech, Flashpoint (a GA Tech accelerator program), and ATDC (one of the country’s oldest and most respected technology incubators). Not a bad place to be if you're learning to code, right?! This central location provides our students with endless opportunities to network with local entrepreneurs, developers, and general supporters of the Atlanta startup scene. As if that isn’t awesome enough on its own, our space is just down the road from great local hotspots like Octane Coffee, Monday Night Brewing, and Ormsby’s.

Also, check out and apply to our satellite campus in Alpharetta, GA, where we offer our Part Time Code Immersion Program a little closer to home for the suburbians! Click here to apply to the Code Immersion Program in Alpharetta. This class is hosted in partnership with Gwinnett Technical College

Classes commence on October 13th.

Applications due September 29th.


For other course offerings in Atlanta or at our satellite campus in Alpharetta, please check out our additional courses in the menu above.

 



 

 

Charlotte

Charlotte may be known as a "banking" city, but we can promise you won't see many ties and sports coats in our offices. Our classes are held in the heart of Uptown at Packard Place. Directly beside the fantastic Romare Bearden Park and the brand new Charlotte Knight's BB&T BallPark, Packard Place is the hub for entrepreneurship and innovation in Charlotte. As the second largest city in the Southeast, opportunities abound in Charlotte. From the big boys (Bank of America, Lowe’s, Duke Energy) to the techies (Skookum, DealCloud, Red Ventures, and so many more) to starting your own thing, Charlotte is a great place to get to work and do something BIG.

Classes commence on October 19th.

Applications due October 5th.


For other course offerings in Charlotte, please check out our additional courses in the menu above.

 



 

 

Dallas

They say "everything is bigger in Texas," right? Perfect! We're looking for people with BIG ideas looking to do BIG things. As the country's fourth-largest employment center, Dallas has got it going on and, TTS is excited to be a part of fueling growth in the tech community. Home to the third-largest concentration of Fortune 500 companies in the nation, Dallas boasts big technology players like Texas Instruments, Intuit, and NetApp alongside awesome startups like TangoTab, Vidpow, and Glass Media. And, hey, the barbecue doesn't hurt, right? We are happy to host our classes out of the "Tech Mecca" itself, Alto 211! Sit in a tire swing, visit with the people of Dallas Fort Work, check out the companies in Tech Wildcatters, and learn to code and do something #dallasbig at our Dallas location.

Classes commence on October 26th.

Applications due October 12th.


 



 

 

Jacksonville

Apply plenty of sunscreen and throw on your hippest wayfarers! That's right. TTS is coming to Florida! The TTS crew is thrilled to call Jacksonville, FL its 7th and newest location. As the largest city by population in the state of Florida, Jacksonville is bustling with all sorts of eager folks looking to learn something new and do something BIG. That's where TTS comes in! Recently ranked 8th in the nation for density concentration of early-stage businesses, Jacksonville is breaking through as a major startup hub in the U.S. Home to events like One Spark (the world's largest crowdfunding festival) and to accelerators like Healthbox, this city is just getting warmed up and TTS is ready to dive in head first.

Classes commence on October 5th.

Applications due September 21st.


 

 
 

 

New Orleans

When we hear New Orleans, the first things we think of are great food, live music, and good times. Oh, and a bustling and growing tech scene, of course! New Orleans is famed for its incredibly rich culture and community and is full of inventive and industrious people looking to do big things in the area. TTS fits right in and, we're thrilled to help foster innovation in the area. In NOLA, we hold our classes out of Propeller, a booming incubator for social innovation and coworking space that's having a big impact in the heart of the city. With everything from more home-grown local tech companies like iSeatz, Federated Sample, and Revelry Labs to global players like General Electric setting up shop in NOLA, it's a great place to be and a great place to learn to code.

Classes commence on October 19th.

Applications due October 5th.

 

 
 

 

Raleigh / Durham / Chapel Hill

Commonly known as “the Triangle,” Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill is home to some of the best universities in the Southeast (including UNC-Chapel Hill, Duke, and NC State University) and as a result...is flooded with smart people doing BIG things. Naturally, TTS wants to put you right smack dab in the middle of the action. With companies like Red Hat, IBM, and Cisco right out our back door and startup activity galore, you’ll be sure to find something right up your alley.

We're thrilled to call HQ our Triangle home.  HQ houses entrepreneurs, change makers, visionaries, and professionals.  We can't imagine a better fit.  

Classes commence on October 13th.

Applications due September 29th.
