Asheville

Asheville - better known amongst the TTS team as "Beer City" - is amazing for so many reasons. With 50 local beers at your fingertips, it's no wonder Asheville has become such a hotspot for techies and start-ups. And if a cold one isn't your thing, the mountains make for beautiful vistas and wicked awesome outdoor activities. Whether you want to jump on with one of Asheville’s awesome web development shops, work alongside other freelance developers living the dream, or start your own thing, Asheville is a great place to begin your journey.

Our full-time classes are hosted out of Mojo Coworking in downtown Asheville and our part-time classes are located in Asheville's Center for Professional Studies.

Applications for the next session are due by 08/24/2014.  

 

 
 

 

Atlanta

Tech Talent South's code immersion programs are hosted in ATDC, one of the country's oldest and most respected technology startup incubators, in the heart of what is known as Technology Square. ATDC's mission is to help technology entrepreneurs in Georgia launch and grow successful companies. In fact, ATDC was recently named by Forbes as one of the "Top 12 Business Incubators Changing the World." Not a bad place to be if you're learning to code, right?! Just down the road is Hypepotamus(a hip popular tech coworking space) and Flashpoint (a GA Tech accelerator program). This central location provides our current students with endless opportunities to network with local entrepreneurs, developers, and general supporters of the Atlanta startup scene. As if that isn't awesome enough on its own, our space just happens to be next door to a Waffle House! "Scattered, smothered, and covered, please!"

Classes commence on 7/28.

 



 

 

Charlotte

Charlotte may be known as a "banking" city, but we can promise you won't see many ties and sports coats in our offices. Our classes are held seconds from Uptown at Industry Charlotte. This dynamic coworking space ensures that our students will be bumping elbows with both established and ambitious entrepreneurs and developers in the community. As the second largest city in the Southeast, opportunities abound in Charlotte. From the big boys (Bank of America, Lowe’s, Duke Energy) to the techies (Skookum, DealCloud, Red Ventures, and so many more) to starting your own thing, Charlotte is a great place to get to work and do something BIG.

 Classes commence on 7/14. 

 

 
 

 

Raleigh/Durham/Chapel Hill

Commonly known as “the Triangle,” Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill is home to some of the best universities in the Southeast (including UNC-Chapel Hill, Duke, and NC State University) and as a result...is flooded with smart people doing BIG things. Naturally, TTS wants to put you right smack dab in the middle of the action. With companies like Red Hat, IBM, and Cisco right out our back door and startup activity galore, you’ll be sure to find something right up your alley.

We're thrilled to call HQ our Triangle home.  HQ houses entrepreneurs, change makers, visionaries, and professionals.  We can't imagine a better fit.  

Classes commence on 7/7.
